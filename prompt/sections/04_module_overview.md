SECTION 4 — Dungeon Module Overview

Purpose of the Section



Describe the dungeon’s overall structure, tone, progression, and thematic intent without room-level specificity.



Strict Rules (Non-Negotiable)



Must define tone and pacing.



Must describe progression from entrance to finale.



Must not include room-specific tactics or encounters.



Required Output Structure



Short thematic overview.



Structural progression description.



Design Constraints



Must support the quest motivation and BBEG.



Avoid repetition of later sections.



Failure Conditions



Inclusion of room-by-room detail.



Tone inconsistent with inputs.
