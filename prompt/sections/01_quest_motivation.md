SECTION 1 — Quest \& Motivation

Purpose of the Section



Define the narrative and logical justification for the adventure, ensuring the party’s involvement is clear, urgent, and meaningful.



Strict Rules (Non-Negotiable)



All five motivations MUST be present:



Why the party goes



Why the party stays



Why the threat must be stopped now



Outcome if the party succeeds



Outcome if the party fails



Content must be readable aloud or paraphrased by a DM.



Must not reference mechanics, statblocks, or map coordinates.



Required Output Structure



Clearly labeled subsections for each motivation element.



Paragraph-based prose, not bullet-only content.



Design Constraints



Stakes must escalate logically.



Consequences must be concrete and regional or personal.



Must align with dungeon theme and BBEG motivation.



Failure Conditions



Missing any of the five required motivations.



Vague or abstract stakes.



Reliance on external lore or IP.
